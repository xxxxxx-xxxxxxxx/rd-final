Final Project

Animals play vital roles in nature, helping maintain ecosystems and biodiversity. They serve as pollinators, predators, and prey, keeping food chains balanced. Many are also domesticated, providing companionship and resources for humans.

Beyond their ecological roles, animals hold cultural and emotional significance. They appear in myths, art, and literature, symbolizing various qualities. Conservation efforts aim to protect endangered species, ensuring future generations can appreciate and benefit from wildlife.
